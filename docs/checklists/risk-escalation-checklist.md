# Risk Escalation Checklist

This checklist is used by the Risk Champion or Project Manager when a risk crosses an escalation threshold and requires immediate attention.

---

## When to Use This Checklist

Escalate a risk when any of the following thresholds are crossed:
- **Probability** increases to High or Critical
- **Impact** increases to High or Critical
- **Mitigation actions** are failing or blocked
- **Timeline** is affected by more than one sprint/iteration
- **Budget** impact exceeds pre-approved variance
- **Stakeholder** concerns escalate beyond normal channels

## Recommended Next Steps

Based on the current risk assessment, recommend one or more of the following:

- [ ] **Accept:** Accept the risk with documented rationale
- [ ] **Mitigate:** Implement additional mitigation actions
- [ ] **Transfer:** Transfer risk to another party (vendor, team, etc.)
- [ ] **Avoid:** Change project scope or approach to eliminate the risk
- [ ] **Escalate Further:** Escalate to executive leadership or steering committee
